What we specialise in

Here at Oregon State Roleplay, we strive to do things right and be "The best of the best". Performing this is extremely hard to achieve, thats why we have listed a few things we have done to allow you to understand the work that has gone into getting this far.

Staff Professionalism

We make sure our members of staff maintain a professional standard, to keep you in good roleplays, not bad ones.

Roleplay Experience

We make sure your roleplay experience is as good as possible. This allows you to be engaged while playing.

Custom Liveries

We have Custom Liveries to emphasise the immersion of being in the State of Oregon. This further allows us to be more realistic, which means you have more fun.

Custom Clothing

We have Custom Clothing to give the immersion of working whether its at the Oregon Fire Station or the Oregon City Police Station, it allows you to equip a uniform.
